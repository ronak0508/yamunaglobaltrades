Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries

How quickly can you help set up our import/export operations?

The timeline varies depending on your specific needs and the countries involved. Typically, we can establish basic operations within 2-4 weeks, with full optimization over the following 2-3 months. During our initial consultation, we'll provide a more specific timeline based on your requirements.

What industries do you specialize in?

We have expertise across numerous industries, including manufacturing, technology, retail, pharmaceuticals, agriculture, automotive, and consumer goods. Our team includes specialists with in-depth knowledge of regulatory requirements and best practices specific to each industry.

Do you work with small businesses or only large corporations?

We work with businesses of all sizes, from startups to multinational corporations. We offer scalable solutions that can be tailored to your specific needs and budget. Our goal is to help businesses of any size succeed in global markets.

How do your fees work?

Our fee structure depends on the services you require. We offer project-based pricing, retainer arrangements, and transaction-based fees. During our initial consultation, we'll discuss your needs and provide transparent pricing options designed to deliver value and ROI for your business.
